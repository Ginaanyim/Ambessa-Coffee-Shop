About Us

Our Story

At Ambessa Coffee, everything starts with passion. We grew up in Eritrea, where coffee is more than just a drink — it’s culture, tradition, and a way to bring people together. From a young age, we were surrounded by the smell, taste, and craft of real coffee.

With hands-on experience in coffee-making and a strong connection to our heritage, we decided to create a company that brings the same quality and care to people everywhere.

What We Do

Ambessa Coffee is a new company with a simple mission: to offer high-quality Ethiopian coffee at a fair price. We source our beans directly from trusted farmers in Ethiopia, focusing on rich flavor, sustainability, and tradition.

Each bag of coffee is carefully selected, roasted, and packed with knowledge and love — from the farm to your cup.

Why Choose Ambessa?

  • Authentic Ethiopian coffee from a trusted source
  • Fair prices without compromising quality
  • Run by passionate founders with real experience
  • A taste of East African tradition in every cup

We are proud of our roots, our journey, and our coffee. With Ambessa Coffee, you are not just buying a product — you’re joining a story.
